Volunteer With Us

Mumbai121 is a not-for-profit initiative that connects fresh talent and persons with benchmark disabilities (PwBD) to startups and small businesses in Mumbai.

Our platform is 100% free for both candidates and companies. It runs with the help of dedicated volunteers who keep the hiring process fair and inclusive.

Are you passionate about making a real impact in people's lives? Do you believe that freshers and persons with benchmark disabilities (PwBD) deserve equal access to paid job opportunities in small companies and startups?

Join us as a volunteer and become a part of this purpose-driven movement!

Why Volunteer With Us?

Freshers and PwBD job seekers often go unnoticed, even as startups and small businesses face a growing demand for talent. We're changing that with a 100% free platform that opens doors to real opportunities and gives them the right break to launch their careers. By volunteering with us, you'll be shaping a fairer job market that empowers individuals, strengthens startups, and drives our nation forward.

What You'll Gain

  • Real Work Experience in recruitment, operations, outreach, marketing, or tech
  • Mentorship from senior professionals
  • Certificate of Contribution after your volunteering period
  • Recognition on our platform (for outstanding contributors)
  • Personal Satisfaction of helping others start their careers

Who Can Volunteer?

We welcome:

  • Experienced or Freshers (preferably with basic computer knowledge)
  • Experienced HR and social work professionals wanting to give back
  • PwBD champions and inclusion advocates
  • Anyone with time, discipline, and a commitment to honest work

Volunteer Roles Available

  • Jobseeker & Employer Outreach
  • WhatsApp & Email Coordination
  • Data Entry and Management (Google Sheets)
  • Social Media Support
  • Website Assistance and Tech Support
  • Mentoring Fresher & PwBD to be Industry Ready
